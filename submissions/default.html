Submissions

Submissions should be sent to:

voice-workshop-2020@cs.toronto.edu

Statements of interest

Statements of interest is a paragraph why one is intereseted in participating in the workshop. This statement of interest can also set the previous work of the attendee in relation to the workshop topic.

Presentation papers

Presentation papers should be 4 to 6 pages long, in the SIGCHI extended abstract format and include a brief statement justifying the fit with the workshop's topic. Summaries of previous research are welcome if they contribute to the workshop's multidisciplinary goals. Submissions will be reviewed according to:

  • Fit with the workshop topic

  • Potential to contribute to the workshop goals

  • A demonstrated track of research in the workshop area (HCI or speech/multimodal/dialogue/conversation processing, with an interest in both areas).

Important Dates:
